**MentAI** is a Swift-based iOS MVP that explores **LLM-assisted wellness insights** and real-time
personalized guidance, built with Cursor and the OpenAI API.

The MVP was pitched to the **Alberta Machine Intelligence Institute (Amii)** — one of Canada's three
federally funded AI research institutes — and to the **University of Alberta Health Sciences Startup
Incubator**.
