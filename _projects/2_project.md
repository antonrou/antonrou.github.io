**Prelude** was built with a team of six at the **Microsoft Global Intern Hackathon**. The system
automatically generates **Model Context Protocol (MCP)** tools for apps in the Microsoft Store
ecosystem by programmatically crawling the operating system's **accessibility tree**, turning any
app's UI surface into agent-callable tooling.

I co-engineered the framework and presented its architecture and live capabilities at the global
hackathon science fair to engineering leadership, product managers, and fellow interns.
